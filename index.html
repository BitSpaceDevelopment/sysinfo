SysInfo

A light weight, free tool to monitor your system in a small Chrome packaged app.

About SysInfo

SysInfo is a small but powerful tool that monitors your computer on any operating system that supports the Chrome web browser.

You can monitor various aspects of your computer including CPU, memory, displays, and browser information. Most of this information is live and will update as stuff happens on your computer.

SysInfo was developed with ChromeOS in mind but is totally functional on any operating system. There are some cool new features coming soon so stay tuned.
